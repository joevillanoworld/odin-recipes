Tofu Katsu Recipe

About this recipe

A crispy treat for me!

Ingredients

  • Tofu, extra firm
  • Cornstarch
  • Panko
  • Hoisin
  • Ketchup
  • Garlic
  • Chili

Let's Get Started

  1. Make the sauce by combining Hoisin, Ketchup, Garlic, and Chili
  2. Press Tofu to remove moisture
  3. Mix cornstarch and water
  4. Tip tofu and coat in Panko
  5. Fry the coated tofu
  6. Serve over rice with sauce
